Case Study 01

A rental operation,
rebuilt as one system.

(01)

The Brief

Project 01 / Ongoing

A multi-location vehicle rental operator was scaling its fleet, investor base, and partner network all at once, with no single source of truth. I architected and built the operating system underneath it: six systems, one flow of data.

The Problem

Growth had outrun the operation's memory.

Every part of the business lived in someone's head, a spreadsheet, or a group chat. No single source of truth, and no way for leadership to see the business without asking someone to go find out.

  • → No real-time visibility into fleet profitability, utilization, or collections. Leadership found out about problems after they'd already cost money.
  • → Vehicle handoff relied entirely on a person remembering every step, with no record if something was missed.
  • → Investor, partner, and renter relationships tangled in one pipeline, with no separation of data or process.
  • → Nothing was logged. Payment failures, stalled pickups, and missed follow-ups left no trace of why.

System 06 · Spotlight

A lead that nurtures itself.

The dedicated lead funnel runs end to end without anyone touching it. A paid ad drives traffic into an isolated landing funnel; the form capture spins up a fully tagged and scored CRM record; and an automated SMS and email sequence nurtures the lead until it converts into a booked call.

Every stage is logged, nothing is re-keyed by hand, and the sales team only sees a lead once it's warm. That's the difference between chasing cold contacts and answering ones that are ready to talk.

(03)

What I did

Dashboard Architecture

Designed and built the ten-KPI executive dashboard from scratch, including status logic, automation rules, and the CEO-facing view, so leadership could assess the entire operation in under two minutes.

Workflow Engineering

Engineered the automated pickup workflows in GHL, covering vehicle auto-fill logic, gated entry conditions, and full event logging, removing manual data entry from the handoff process entirely.

Forms & Funnel Builds

Designed and built the intake forms and multi-step funnels behind the lead-gen system, from field-level validation to the automated tagging and routing that fires on submission.

Risk Catch

Ran a deep architectural review that surfaced a missing link between renter and vehicle records, a gap that would have caused silent reporting errors downstream. Flagged and scoped the fix before it shipped.

(04)

What changed

0 KPIs

Leadership now tracks fleet performance, collections, and utilization in a live 10-metric dashboard, instead of finding out about problems weeks later.

0 Manual

Vehicle handoff runs on a fully gated, event-logged workflow with zero manual data entry, replacing a process that lived in one person's memory.

Pre-launch

A structural gap between renter and vehicle records was caught during architecture review and fixed before go-live, not after it became a reporting error.

Clean split

Investors, partners, and renters now each run on their own fully isolated CRM pipeline, replacing one tangled system where everything was mixed.
